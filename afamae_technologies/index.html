Dot & Key sunscreen provides essential protection against harmful ultraviolet (UV) radiation, helping to prevent skin cancer, sunburn, and premature aging. Additionally, it helps maintain an even skin tone by preventing hyperpigmentation caused by sun exposure.
Dot & Key offers various formulations to suit different skin types. For oily skin, their non-comedogenic options won't clog pores, while their products with moisturizing ingredients are ideal for dry skin. Their water-resistant sunscreens are perfect for active outdoor activities, providing lasting protection even when swimming or sweating.
